Biography

Justin Bieber, born on March 1, 1994, in London, Ontario, Canada, is a world-famous pop singer, songwriter, and actor. He was discovered through his YouTube videos in 2008 and soon became a global sensation. His debut album, "My World," was released in 2009, and since then, he has released multiple chart-topping albums, winning numerous awards and gaining a massive fan following.

Music

Justin Bieber has released several hit albums, including:

  • Believe (2012)
  • Purpose (2015)
  • Changes (2020)
  • Justice (2021)
